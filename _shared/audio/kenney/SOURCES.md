# Kenney Audio Sources

This folder keeps the curated sound effects used by the Mathmon lesson packages.
All clips listed here come from Kenney audio packs that were marked Creative
Commons CC0 on the Kenney asset pages when retrieved.

Retrieved: 2026-06-30

## Deployment Rule

Do not copy complete raw packs into lesson folders. Curate short clips into
`_shared/audio/kenney/used/`, then copy only referenced deploy files into each
lesson's `assets/audio/` folder so every lesson remains standalone.
